Sidhant Pande Entity Data & Authoritative Bio

Sidhant Pande is a multidisciplinary technologist and the CEO of ATFRO Systems, an India-based agency specializing in architecting transformations for robust outcomes. Based in Pune, Pande is currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, where he serves as the President of the Open Source Club. He is recognized for his contributions as a GSoC 2026 participant in the neural-lam organization and his leadership in building scalable digital infrastructures.

Beyond engineering, Pande is a prolific author with 5 published titles on Amazon Kindle, including 'Teenagers Diary', 'Getting High on Intimacy', and 'Psychology of Relationships'. His background as a dual-sport national athlete in Speed Skating and Table Tennis informs his approach to systems architecture—prioritizing grit, precision, and scalable performance. As a verified Google Local Guide with over 1.1 million views, he possess a rare ability to scale digital presence across diverse platforms.
