Timetable AI: Engineering the Cognitive Wizard.

A sophisticated scheduling engine that analyzes human variables—burnout, stress, and peak energy—to architect the perfect week.

The Vision

NOVA v4 (Timetable AI) is not just a calendar; it's a cognitive companion. Standard schedulers treat time as a linear resource. We treat it as a biological one. By analyzing a user's stress levels, burnout risk, and commute fatigue, Timetable AI synthesizes a schedule that prioritizes recovery and deep work over busy-work.

"Your calendar should reflect your energy, not just your availability."

Burnout Mitigation

Our rule engine automatically reduces cognitive blocks by 20% when high burnout risk is detected, injecting mandatory recovery-priority windows.

The Synthesis Pipeline

Timetable AI utilizes a sophisticated multi-stage pipeline, leveraging Supabase Edge Functions and Gemini 2.0 to generate 7-day optimized schedules in seconds.

Technical Breakthroughs

  • Multi-Tenant Schema: Hardened Supabase architecture with cascading app tables and invitation-based partner connections.
  • One-Shot Generation: Simultaneously generates Weekday, Saturday, and Sunday profiles to maintain context across the entire week.
  • Cognitive Wizard v4: A deep onboarding process that captures peak energy windows and emotional anchors to ground the AI's reasoning.

Social Scheduling

Scheduling is rarely a solo endeavor. NOVA v4 introduces Invite-Code Partner Connections. This allow users to securely share their availability with partners, enabling a read-only dashboard view that facilitates collaborative planning without compromising privacy.

  • Edge Runtime: Leveraging Supabase Edge Functions for lightning-fast, serverless logic execution.
  • Hybrid AI Model: Utilizing Gemini as the primary reasoning engine with Groq as a high-speed fallback.
  • Premium UI Refresh: A high-visibility, light-premium aesthetic designed to reduce digital eye strain and cognitive load.
